02.

Don't try to break the bot. Spamming commands to inflate your numbers or attempting to bypass rate limits will result in a global blacklist. Play fair, stay on the leaderboard.

Message Counter is provided "as-is." While we aim for 24/7 uptime, we are not liable for data loss due to Discord API outages or server migrations. Stats are precious, but they aren't legal currency.
